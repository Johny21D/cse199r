Australian Meat Pie

Region: Oceania (Australia)

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground beef
  • 1 Onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Beef stock and Cornstarch (for thick gravy)
  • Shortcrust pastry (base) & Puff pastry (lid)

Instructions

  1. Brown beef and onions; add stock and sauces. Simmer until thick.
  2. Line a pie tin with shortcrust pastry and fill with meat mixture.
  3. Place puff pastry on top and seal the edges.
  4. Bake at 200°C for 25 minutes until the lid is golden and flaky.

Grilled Barramundi

Region: Oceania (Australia)

Ingredients

  • 2 Barramundi fillets (skin on)
  • Lemon myrtle (optional indigenous herb)
  • Olive oil and Sea salt
  • Garlic butter and Parsley
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Pat the skin dry and score it slightly with a knife.
  2. Sear skin-side down in a hot pan with oil until crispy (about 4 mins).
  3. Flip and cook for 2 more minutes with garlic butter.
  4. Serve with a sprinkle of lemon myrtle and fresh lemon.

Australian Roast Lamb

Region: Oceania (Australia)

Ingredients

  • 2kg Leg of Lamb
  • 4 cloves Garlic, slivered
  • Fresh Rosemary sprigs
  • Olive oil, Salt, and Pepper
  • Potatoes, Pumpkins, and Carrots (for roasting)

Instructions

  1. Make small slits in the lamb and insert garlic and rosemary.
  2. Rub with oil and salt; place in a roasting pan with vegetables.
  3. Roast at 180°C for approx. 1 hour 15 mins (for medium-rare).
  4. Let the meat rest for 15 minutes before carving.

Classic Pavlova

Region: Oceania (Australia)

Ingredients

  • 4 Egg whites
  • 1 cup Caster sugar
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch and 1 tsp White vinegar
  • Whipped cream
  • Passionfruit, Strawberries, and Kiwi fruit

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg whites to stiff peaks, then gradually add sugar until glossy.
  2. Fold in cornstarch and vinegar.
  3. Shape into a circle on a baking tray; bake at 120°C for 90 mins.
  4. Let cool completely in the oven; top with cream and fruit.
