Japanese Sushi Rolls (Maki)

Region: East Asia

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sushi rice (vinegared)
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Fresh Salmon or Tuna (Sashimi grade)
  • Cucumber and Avocado strips
  • Soy sauce, Wasabi, and Ginger

Instructions

  1. Spread rice thinly over the Nori sheet.
  2. Place fish and vegetables in a line across the center.
  3. Roll tightly using a bamboo mat.
  4. Slice into bite-sized pieces with a sharp, wet knife.

Indian Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Region: South Asia

Ingredients

  • 800g Chicken thighs, boneless
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt & Lemon juice
  • Spices: Garam Masala, Turmeric, Cumin, Chili
  • 1 cup Tomato puree & 1/2 cup Heavy cream
  • Large knob of Butter

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices; grill until charred.
  2. Simmer tomato puree with butter and more spices for 15 mins.
  3. Stir in cream and the grilled chicken.
  4. Serve hot with Garlic Naan or Basmati rice.

Beef Lok Lak

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • 400g Beef tenderloin, cubed
  • Oyster sauce, Soy sauce, and Sugar
  • Lettuce leaves, Tomato slices, Red onion
  • Dipping Sauce: Lime juice, Sea salt, and Kampot Pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in oyster sauce and soy sauce for 30 minutes.
  2. Flash-fry the beef in a very hot wok until browned but tender.
  3. Arrange fresh vegetables on a plate and place beef on top.
  4. Serve with the lime and pepper dipping sauce on the side.

Korean Bibimbap

Region: East Asia

Ingredients

  • Warm white rice
  • Saut√©ed Spinach, Carrots, and Shiitake mushrooms
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Bulgogi Beef or Fried Egg
  • Toasted Sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Place rice in a bowl and arrange colorful vegetables on top.
  2. Add the meat or fried egg in the center.
