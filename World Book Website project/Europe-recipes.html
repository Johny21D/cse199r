French Beef Bourguignon

Region: Western Europe

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg Beef chuck, cubed
  • 200g Bacon lardons
  • 750ml Red Wine (Burgundy)
  • Carrots, Onions, and Mushrooms
  • Fresh Thyme and Bay Leaves

Instructions

  1. Brown the beef and bacon in a large pot.
  2. Add vegetables and sauté until softened.
  3. Pour in wine and stock; simmer low for 3 hours.
  4. Add sautéed mushrooms in the last 20 minutes.

Italian Lasagna Classico

Region: Southern Europe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • 500g Ground Beef & Pork mix
  • 700ml Tomato Passata
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk)
  • Parmesan and Mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook a thick meat ragù with tomatoes and herbs.
  2. Layer: Sauce, Pasta, Meat, Béchamel, and Cheese.
  3. Repeat until the dish is full.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 45 minutes until bubbly.

German Pork Schnitzel

Region: Central Europe

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork cutlets (pounded thin)
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups Breadcrumbs (Panko)
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Dredge meat in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry in hot oil/butter for 3 minutes per side.
  3. Ensure the crust is golden and "souffléd" (bubbly).
  4. Serve immediately with fresh lemon.

Swedish Meatballs (Köttbullar)

Region: Northern Europe

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground Beef/Pork mix
  • 1/2 cup Breadcrumbs soaked in milk
  • Allspice and Nutmeg
  • Beef stock & Heavy cream (for gravy)
  • Lingonberry jam (side)

Instructions

  1. Mix meat, spices, and soaked bread; roll into small balls.
  2. Fry in butter until browned and cooked through.
  3. In the same pan, whisk flour, stock, and cream for gravy.
  4. Toss meatballs back in the sauce and serve with mash.
