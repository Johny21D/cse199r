French Beef Bourguignon

Region: Western Europe

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg Beef chuck, cubed
  • 200g Bacon lardons
  • 750ml Red Wine (Burgundy)

Instructions

  1. Brown beef and bacon; sauté vegetables.
  2. Simmer in wine for 3 hours.

Italian Lasagna Classico

Region: Southern Europe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • 500g Ground Beef & Pork mix
  • Béchamel sauce & Passata

Instructions

  1. Cook thick ragù; layer with pasta.
  2. Bake at 180°C for 45 mins.

German Pork Schnitzel

Region: Central Europe

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork cutlets (thin)
  • Flour, Eggs, Breadcrumbs
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Bread meat; fry in oil until golden.
  2. Serve hot with fresh lemon.
