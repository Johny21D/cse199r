Fish Amok (Amok Trei)

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • 500g White fish fillets
  • 3 tbsp Khmer Kroeung (Herb paste)
  • 1 cup Coconut cream & 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp Fish sauce & Palm sugar
  • Noni leaves or Cabbage

Instructions

  1. Blend Kroeung paste with coconut cream and egg until smooth.
  2. Gently fold in the fish chunks to coat them in the curry.
  3. Steam in banana leaf cups for 20-30 minutes until set.
  4. Garnish with kaffir lime leaves and sliced red chilies.

Beef Lok Lak

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • 400g Beef tenderloin, cubed
  • Oyster sauce, Soy sauce, and Sugar
  • Lettuce leaves, Tomato slices, Red onion
  • Dipping Sauce: Lime juice, Sea salt, and Kampot Pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in oyster sauce and soy sauce for 30 minutes.
  2. Flash-fry the beef in a very hot wok until browned but tender.
  3. Arrange fresh vegetables on a plate and place beef on top.
  4. Serve with the lime and pepper dipping sauce on the side.

Kuy Teav (Noodle Soup)

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Pork bone broth
  • Ground pork and Shrimp
  • Fried garlic, Bean sprouts, and Lime

Instructions

  1. Prepare a clear broth using pork bones and dried squid.
  2. Blanch noodles and place in a bowl with cooked meats.
  3. Pour hot broth over the top and add fried garlic oil.
  4. Serve with a side of herbs and lime wedges.

Nom Banh Chok (Khmer Noodles)

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • Fresh rice noodles
  • Green fish curry (Lemongrass & Turmeric)
  • Cucumber, Mint, and Basil
  • Shredded banana flower

Instructions

  1. Pound fish meat with Kroeung paste and simmer in coconut milk.
  2. Place a mound of fresh noodles in a bowl.
  3. Ladle the green fish gravy over the noodles.
  4. Top with a mountain of fresh raw vegetables and herbs.

Samlor Korko

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • Catfish or Pork belly
  • Toasted ground rice (for thickening)
  • Green papaya, Pumpkin, and Eggplant
  • Kroeung paste and Prahok

Instructions

  1. Fry the paste and meat together until fragrant.
  2. Add water and hard vegetables; simmer until tender.
  3. Stir in the toasted rice to thicken the soup.
  4. Add leafy greens at the end and serve with rice.

Lort Cha (Short Fat Noodles)

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • Short pin noodles (Lort)
  • Beef slices and Bean sprouts
  • Chinese broccoli
  • Fried egg and Sweet soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry beef and broccoli in a hot wok with garlic.
  2. Add noodles and soy sauce mixture; toss at high heat.
  3. Mix in bean sprouts for the final 30 seconds.
  4. Top with a sunny-side-up egg and chili sauce.

Kep Stir-Fried Crab

Region: Coastal Cambodia

Ingredients

  • 2 Fresh blue crabs
  • 3 tbsp Fresh Kampot green peppercorns
  • Garlic, Spring onions
  • Oyster sauce and Palm sugar

Instructions

  1. Clean and crack the crab shells slightly.
  2. Sauté garlic and green peppercorns in oil until fragrant.
  3. Add crab and stir-fry at high heat with sauces.
  4. Toss until the crab is red and the peppercorns are softened.

Prahok Ktiss

Region: Central Cambodia

Ingredients

  • 200g Minced pork
  • 2 tbsp Prahok (fermented fish paste)
  • 1 cup Coconut cream & Pea eggplants
  • Red Kroeung paste

Instructions

  1. Fry Kroeung paste in coconut cream until oil rises.
  2. Add minced pork and Prahok; stir until cooked.
  3. Drop in pea eggplants and simmer until soft.
  4. Serve as a dip with fresh cucumbers, carrots, and cabbage.

Chaa Kh'nhei (Ginger Stir-fry)

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken pieces
  • 2 cups Fresh ginger (julienned)
  • Soy sauce & Fish sauce
  • Fresh bird's eye chilies

Instructions

  1. Fry ginger in oil until it becomes golden and crispy.
  2. Add chicken and stir-fry until fully cooked.
  3. Season with soy sauce, sugar, and chilies.
  4. Serve hot—the dish should be very spicy and ginger-heavy.

Samlor Machu Yuon

Region: Lower Mekong

Ingredients

  • Fish or Shrimp
  • Pineapple chunks & Tomatoes
  • Lotus root or Celery
  • Tamarind juice & Rice paddy herb

Instructions

  1. Boil water with tamarind and fish sauce.
  2. Add fish and pineapple; simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and lotus root at the end.
  4. Garnish with fried garlic and fresh rice paddy herbs.

Bok Svay (Mango Salad)

Region: Southeast Asia (Cambodia)

Ingredients

  • 1 Large green mango (shredded)
  • Dried shrimp or Smoked fish
  • Shallots, Peanuts, and Mint
  • Fish sauce & Lime dressing

Instructions

  1. Shred the green mango into thin long strips.
  2. Toss with shallots, dried shrimp, and herbs.
  3. Drizzle with a mixture of fish sauce, sugar, and lime.
  4. Top with crushed roasted peanuts before serving.

Num Ansom Chek

Region: National (Festive)

Ingredients

  • Sticky rice
  • Small sweet bananas
  • Coconut milk & Salt
  • Banana leaves (for wrapping)

Instructions

  1. Soak sticky rice and mix with coconut milk.
  2. Place rice on banana leaf, add banana in the center.
  3. Wrap tightly and tie with bamboo strings.
  4. Boil the packages for 4–6 hours until tender.
