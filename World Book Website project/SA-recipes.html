Culinary Wonders of South America

Colombian/Venezuelan Arepas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Cornmeal (P.A.N.)
  • 2 ½ cups Warm water
  • 1 tsp Salt & 1 tbsp Oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix water, salt, and oil. Add cornmeal.
  2. Shape into discs.
  3. Sear for 5-7 mins per side.

Peruvian Ceviche

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb White fish (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Lime juice
  • Red onion & Habanero

Instructions:

  1. Season fish with salt/chili.
  2. Add onions and lime juice.
  3. Marinate 5–10 mins and serve.

Brazilian Brigadeiros

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz Condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 3 tbsp Cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Cook ingredients until thick.
  2. Cool on a greased plate.
  3. Roll into balls and sprinkle.

Argentine Asado

Ingredients:

  • Skirt Steak
  • Coarse salt
  • Chimichurri sauce

Instructions:

  1. Salt the meat.
  2. Grill on high heat.
  3. Serve with chimichurri.
